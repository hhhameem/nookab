## 4. Features Section

### Suggested

| #   | Feature                             | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| --- | ----------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | **Mastery-Based Spaced Repetition** | Before each practice session, the engine classifies your words into buckets based on how well you know them. Words at risk of forgetting surface first, so you always practice what matters most. Unlike traditional SRS that locks words until their due date, **Nookab never blocks you from practicing — the algorithm suggests, not commands.**                              |
| 2   | **Weak Word Detection**             | A word is flagged weak when you answer it incorrectly twice in a row, or when your accuracy drops below 60% after 5+ reviews. Weak words are automatically prioritized in practice sessions until they recover. To recover, a word needs 5 consecutive correct answers spanning at least two different days — **we make sure when you master a word, you actually mastered it.** |
| 3   | **Detailed Statistics**             | Track your accuracy by word type, category, and over time. See your hardest words, most practiced, and activity over 14 days. Charts show progress at a glance — no need to dig through menus. **Everything is computed locally, so stats load instantly even in airplane mode.**                                                                                                |
| 4   | **CSV Import/Export**               | Your vocabulary is yours — even if you leave. Export all your words before you go, and if you ever come back, import them and voila — everything is exactly where you left it. **Full data portability. No data loss, no starting over.**                                                                                                                                        |
| 5   | **Quick Sprint**                    | Four one-tap practice shortcuts on the dashboard — no setup required. **Quick Sprint** auto-starts a 5-question Quiz from all your words. **Today's Words** focuses on words you added today. **Mastery Boost** targets low-mastery words. **Clear Weak Words** hones in on weak spots. Each shortcut has their own daily limits to keep practice focused and intentional.       |
| 6   | **Consistency Streaks**             | Track your daily learning streak with visual weekly dots. Active days are filled; inactive days stay muted. Build habits that stick with daily goals and progress tracking. **The streak system uses a 7-day window — miss a day and the streak resets, creating a gentle accountability loop.**                                                                                 |
| 7   | **German Special Characters**       | Floating keyboard helper for ä, ö, ü, ß, Ä, Ö, Ü. **Solves a specific input problem for German learners on non-German keyboards — reduces typing time from 1.5-2.5s per character (long-press menu) to ~0.3s (single tap).**                                                                                                                                                     |
| 8   | **Text-to-Speech**                  | Hear pronunciation in German (de-DE) and English (en-US). Tap the speaker icon on any word to hear it spoken aloud. **Uses dual-coding theory — information presented through both visual and auditory channels is retained better than either alone.** Meta-analyses estimate 15-25% retention improvement with audio reinforcement.                                            |
| 9   | **Language Tips**                   | Language-specific learning advice for English and German learners. **Practical tips that help you understand grammar patterns, common mistakes, and cultural context.** Daily insights — master the language, one tip at a time.                                                                                                                                                 |
| 10  | **Daily Targets**                   | Set a daily practice goal and track your progress throughout the day. The dashboard shows a circular progress ring that fills as you practice. **Set it once, hit it every day.**                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| 11  | **Category Management**             | Organize vocabulary into semantic groups like "Travel," "Food," or "Business." **Categories aid memory via chunking — words learned in a categorical context are recalled better when the same category is cued during practice.** Create, edit, or delete categories anytime.                                                                                                   |
| 12  | **14-Day Activity Chart**           | Visual bar graph showing your daily practice over the last two weeks. **See patterns in your learning habits — which days you practiced most, which days you skipped.** Uses your configured week start day (Monday or Sunday) so the chart matches your calendar.                                                                                                               |

## 5. How It Works Section

### Suggested

**Headline:** Your Words. Your Way.

**Step Descriptions:**

1. **Add Words** — Encounter a new word? Tap + and add it with translation, article, and category.
2. **Learn Words** — Choose Flashcard, Quiz, or Spelling mode. The smart engine picks words that need attention.
3. **Track Progress** — Watch words move from Learning to Mastered. Weak words are flagged automatically. Mastered words get a trophy.
4. **Master Words** — Words need 5 consecutive correct answers across 2 days to be mastered. No shortcuts.

## 6. Practice Modes Section

### Suggested

**Headline:** Every word, every way.

**Subtitle:** 3 practice modes. Unlimited ways to start.

**Practice Modes:**

1. **Flashcards** — See word, flip to reveal all its details at once — translation, article, examples, notes. Review everything about a word in one glance.
2. **Quiz** — Pick the correct translation from four options. Tests recognition — can you identify the right word from similar alternatives?
3. **Spelling** — Type the word from memory with no hints. The most challenging mode — full recall from scratch. Strongest evidence of true retention.

**Quick Practice Shortcuts:**
Skip the setup — one tap and you're practicing.

1. **Quick Sprint** — 5-question Quiz from all words
2. **Today's Words** — Focus on words you added today
3. **Mastery Boost** — Target low-mastery words
4. **Clear Weak Words** — Hone in on weak spots
